    <h2>How to Play</h2>
    <ul>
        <li>Start the game and enter the Two-Player mode from the main menu.</li>
        <li>Player 1 will play as 'X', and Player 2 will play as 'O'.</li>
        <li>Players take turns placing their marks on the 3x3 grid.</li>
        <li>The first player to get three of their marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins the game.</li>
        <li>If all nine squares are filled and no player has three in a row, the game is declared a draw.</li>
    </ul>
